# The Seductive Trap of Bad Habits
- **ID:** 202601221030
- **Date:** 2026-01-22
- **Tags:** #habits #delayed-gratification #decision-making #human-behavior
- **Source:** [[Brian Tracy - Habla menos actua mas]]

## Idea
I don't know why our mind always tends to feel seduced by bad habits. Bad habits have that spark that attracts us and makes us do things we know aren't the right path, but still, the reward of falling into a bad habit is so high that most human beings prefer to take that path rather than choose a good habit, even knowing deep down that in the medium-long term the good habit will bring us greater rewards and, ultimately, a more lasting happiness over time.
